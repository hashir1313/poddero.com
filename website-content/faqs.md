## Questions

### 1. What podcast services do you provide?

We offer podcast editing, audio enhancement, content repurposing, guest booking, distribution, and marketing support.

### 2. Which podcast platforms do you support?

We help distribute podcasts to major platforms including Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, Amazon Music, and more.

### 3. How long does it take to deliver an episode?

Turnaround times vary by project scope, but most episodes are delivered within a few business days.

### 4. Do I keep full ownership of my content?

Yes. You retain complete ownership of your podcast, recordings, and all published content.

### 5. Can you work with an existing podcast?

Absolutely. Whether you're launching a new show or improving an established one, our services can be tailored to your needs.

### 6. What do I need to provide?

Simply send us your recordings and any relevant notes. We'll handle the technical production and optimization process.

### 7. Do you offer custom packages?

Yes. We create customized solutions based on your podcast's goals, publishing frequency, and service requirements.

### 8. How do I get started?

Book a consultation with our team, and we'll discuss your podcast, goals, and the best plan for your growth.
